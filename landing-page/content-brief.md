## Content Tone Guidelines

**Voice:** Direct. Slightly provocative. Emotionally honest. Like a trusted friend who tells you the truth you are avoiding.

**Sentence Length:** Short. Declarative. Fragment-friendly where it adds punch.

**Philosophy:** Stoic minimalism meets modern product design. Time is the most valuable resource. Awareness creates urgency. Urgency creates intention.

**What to avoid:**
- Hype language ("revolutionary," "game-changing," "amazing")
- Exclamation marks (the content should create its own energy)
- Corporate tone (this is a personal app, not enterprise software)
- Guilt-tripping (inspire action, do not shame inaction)
- Emoji in copy (the design and typography carry the emotion)

**What to lean into:**
- Specific numbers (16,060 days, 4,000 weeks, 45 summers)
- Contrast (abstract vs. concrete, feeling vs. knowing)
- Second person ("you," "your" -- always speaking to the reader)
- Brevity as emphasis (one-sentence paragraphs that land hard)
